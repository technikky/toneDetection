Sight-Singing Studio — Voice Recording & Music Practice Consent

[SCHOOL / MUSIC PROGRAM NAME] · [TEACHER NAME] · [SCHOOL YEAR]

Our music class uses Sight-Singing Studio, an offline software application that runs entirely on the classroom computer with no internet connection. To give your child feedback on their singing, the application:

  • Records short (a few seconds to a minute) audio clips of your child singing an assigned exercise.
  • Analyzes the pitch and pronunciation of that recording to produce a practice score.
  • Stores the recording and score locally on the classroom computer, associated with an access code assigned to your child by the teacher -- your child does not create an account or type their own name into the application.

The recordings and scores stay on this computer. They are not uploaded to the internet, sold, or shared with any third party. See the attached Privacy Policy for full details.
